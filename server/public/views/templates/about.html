About Fussbudget

Caregiver Scheduling

Fussbudget is a lightweight, mobile-first scheduling webapp that helps caregivers stay on top of the daily schedules of their charges and adapts to the unique challenges each day presents.

Smart Defaults

Fussbudget builds out a rough default schedule based on the client's date of birth. Caregivers can modify each client's individual schedule manually, and save that as the client's default schedule to be loaded on each day's login.

Wakes Up With You

Users can change the day's wake-up time, and Fussbudget will dynamically rebuild the client's schedule for the day. If the schedule gets too packed, Fussbudget will remove lower-priority events automatically.

Technologies
